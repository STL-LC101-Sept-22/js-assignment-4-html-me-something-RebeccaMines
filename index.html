My Adventure At The Renaissance Festival

During the summer of 2022, I went on an adventure to The Renaissance Festival with some of my best friends. It was one of the most memerable adventures I've ever had and I'd love to share the memories with you.

Jayda: left, Brittany: middle, Me: right

There were a lot of cool things for us to see! Rows of venders to shop from, large booths where special events could take place, and everybody was dressed up in armor and tattered clothing! I even found a man with a very large dragon puppet that let me take a photo with him, how cool!
